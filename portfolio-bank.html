About the Project

Every Indian nationalised bank has a policy and promise to provide their employees the best experience of using latest furniture and computer technology while they work and serve their end customers with loyality and trust.
To build and fulfill the promise, banks auction their old technology, computer assets, furniture every 6-7 years, buying new stuff for their employees.

My Role

I being Founder of the Application, Developed, sold and marketed an application called "Bankscrap", which helped the banks to advertise anything they want to sell, with a single click.
Aided the end customer, business owners to be notified when anything of their interest comes in, bid for it and get the stuff they need, creating a win-win situation for either parties.
This application targetted more than 190 branches, and was growing, hitting a mark of 0.1M Dollars (more than 80 Lac Rs.) profit, in a span of an year
